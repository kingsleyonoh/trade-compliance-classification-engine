# Product Baseline — Trade Compliance Classification Engine

## Users
Import-heavy distributors, marketplaces, freight-forwarding compliance teams, e-commerce operators, classifiers, reviewers, auditors, and admins.

## Positioning
Dense compliance workbench for repeatable customs decisions. The product should feel evidence-led, structured, auditable, and calm under uncertainty.

## Trust Requirements
- Always show rule-pack version, evidence, rejected alternatives, confidence, risk band, and reviewer history.
- Make optional integrations visibly non-blocking.
- Never imply legal advice or guaranteed customs correctness.
- Preserve original machine decisions after override.

## Anti-References
No generic SaaS marketing gloss, no playful customs metaphors, no hidden security-by-UI controls, no RAG-first experience.
